    Conditions of Adoption

    A cat is a living being with an average lifespan of 15 years. To adopt a cat is to get into a long-term commitment and as such, there are conditions to the adoption.

    Breeding Birmans is our passion and we care deeply about our cats and kittens. When sending a kitten to its forever home, we must not regret it and feel like it was a mistake. The kittens' well-being is our top priority.

    Therefore, we reserve the right to refuse an adoption if we deem it necessary.

    Our kittens will leave the cattery between 12 and 16 weeks old with:

    • health record
    • up-to-date vaccines
    • dewormed
    • certification of a good health issued by a veterinarian
    • adoption contract
    • being spayed/neutered
    • pedigree (proof of registration)
    • copy of parent's genetic testing
    • microchip
    • kitten kit

    PRICE

    The price varies between 1200€ and 1800€

    The exact price is determined according to the cat's color and whether it is a show quality or a pet quality cat.

    The price includes vaccination, deworming, sterilization, pedigree and a kitten kit.

    We understand that a purebred cat represents a cost. Therefore, it is possible to pay in several instalments. The cat will remain a property of the cattery until the whole balance is paid.

    PROCESS OF ADOPTION

    Research the breed and cat ownership, if it is your first time owning a cat. Make sure that a Birman is the right cat for you and that you can give it the proper care it deserves. Figure out which gender and color you prefer in your kitten.

    Choose a kitten. This can be done either by checking our available kittens, or by contacting us - we can tell you about the kittens in the cattery, when are the next litters planned, write down your ideal kitten and let you know when we get one.

    Contact us. Tell us which kitten you would like to adopt. If it is available, you will be able to make a soft reservation.

    To adopt a kitten, it is a requirement to visit the cattery at least once. It is both for us to know the person adopting, and for you to see the cattery and meet your possible future cat.

    After the visit, you will be able to reserve the kitten. A deposit of 300€ is required to complete the reservation. The deposit is non-returnable if the reservation is cancelled less than 2 weeks before the kitten is supposed to leave.

    When it is the time for the kitten to leave, you will have to come pick it up - we don't send our kittens through 3rd parties. At the cattery, you will pay the remaining balance and sign the adoption contract, upon which the kitten is officially yours! You will leave with your kitten, its certificates and a kitten kit.

    About Us

    I AM MARIE BLANCHO

    and I am the owner of this small family cattery. Birman cats have been my passion for more than 18 years, and to live surrounded by my four-legged friends, my sacred cats of Burma, brings me joy every day.
    My goal is to be a part of building and improving this very special breed.

    I carry only a few litters per year, as | wish to remain a family breeder for the happiness of my sacred Birman females, and thus give my kittens the best possible start to their lives.

    Our kingdom is located in Boissy St. Léger, Val de Marne (94)


    WHY THE SACRED CAT OF BURMA?

    The reason is simple - we love this breed, its character and its beauty.
    I first encountered Birman cats on vacation. The moment | saw a Birman cat l fell in love with it. And as soon as Lhad the opportunity, l bought a little Seal Point kitten. | did not know the criteria for choosing a Sacred Birman at that time. But Lwas lucky, he turned out to be a superb quality Birman and that's how | started my exhibition career.

    Over the time | presented with many Birmans, one of which got the highest title Great European Champion).

    Eventually, | began with breeding. My objective was always to remain a family breeder, in order to share the same affection with all my companions, and of course their kittens so that they make their new owners happy.

    WHY "ROYAUME DE NEFER"?

    The journey of this kingdom began in Egypt, where I first encountered the Sacred cat of Burma.
    As we wanted to pay homage to this place, we decided to name our cattery Kingdom of Nefer/Royaume de Nefer, referencing the great ancient queens of Egypt, Nefertiti and Nefertari.

    When a kitten is born in the kingdom, | try to give them a name of a King or a Queen or a deity, thus matching the affix. For example, in the year of the R, we had Rama (Indian king) and Rhea (Jordanian queen). For letter S we had Sisi (beautiful empress in a white dress"). Other kittens born were named Venerha, Ramses, etc.
